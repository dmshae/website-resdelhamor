About Us

Nestled in the breathtaking province of Sorsogon, Philippines, Recidencia Del Hamor offers a peaceful retreat for travelers seeking tranquility and relaxation. With a prime location and easy access to the region's stunning natural attractions, it serves as the perfect base for exploring Sorsogon’s beauty. From the moment you arrive, you’ll be greeted with a warm and welcoming atmosphere. Our friendly staff is always available to assist with any requests, ensuring your stay is memorable. Recidencia Del Hamor provides a comfortable, laid-back environment where you can unwind and reconnect with nature. Whether you are enjoying serene moments or discovering local sights, we are dedicated to providing an authentic and enjoyable experience.
